# Embers - Ed-Tech Venture Exploration

**Status:** Early Research
**Stage:** Market Research & Network Building
**Focus:** AI-powered educational transformation

## Overview

Research and exploration project investigating opportunities in AI-enabled educational technology. Inspired by transformative educational experiences (Black Butte School, Semester at Sea) and the potential for AI to revolutionize how children learn.

## Key Insights

### Market Validation
- **#1 ChatGPT use case: Tutoring** - Top 3 use cases all around self-education
- Massive demand for personalized, AI-assisted learning
- Growing movement toward alternative education models

### Schools & Programs of Interest

**Innovative Educational Models:**
- **Alpha School** (Arizona) - 2-hour learning model, rapid growth
- **Brave Generation Academy** (Tanzania) - High school program at base of Mt. Kilimanjaro
- **Lumiar School** (Brazil) - Ricardo Semler's radical approach to education
- **Prequel** (Arizona) - Kids building real companies
- **Humin University** - EQ-focused curriculum development

**Tools & Platforms:**
- **Lovable.dev** - Rapid prototyping for education (used with kids)
- **Magic School** - AI tools for educators
- **Google Labs** - Turning any content into tailored education
- **StarSpark.ai** - Education technology innovation
